## Features
- Branded online ordering page (delivery + pickup)
- Tablet-ready POS system for dine-in
- Real-time kitchen display system (KDS)
- Digital menu management with modifiers & variants
- Table management with QR code ordering
- Reservation system
- Delivery tracking & driver management
- Payment processing (cash, card, online via Stripe)
- Tip management & split bills
- Operating hours & availability scheduling
- Promotion & coupon system
- Customer database & order history
- Sales analytics & popular item reports
- Review & rating management
- Multi-location support
- Real-time order notifications via WebSocket
